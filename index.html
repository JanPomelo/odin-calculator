How to use the Calculator

This calculator can solve basic mathematical Problems for you. You can use it either by clicking on the Buttons on the Website or by pressing the keys on your keyboards. The list for the special Button and their Keybinding is below this text. Have fun calculating! PS: Don't forget to shut down the Calculator to save its Batteries! ;)
